# Product Overview

## Community Manager Inteligente Course

This is a Spanish-language online course landing page for "Community Manager Inteligente con IA" (Intelligent Community Manager with AI), created by SEO Contenidos, a Venezuelan marketing agency with 11+ years of experience.

### Key Product Details
- **Target Market**: Spanish-speaking community managers, entrepreneurs, and digital marketing professionals
- **Price**: $47 USD (discounted from $197)
- **Format**: Self-paced online course with lifetime access
- **Content**: 11 modules covering AI-powered social media management
- **Certification**: Digital certificate upon completion

### Value Proposition
The course teaches traditional community managers how to integrate AI tools into their workflow to:
- Create strategic content faster
- Automate research and competitor analysis
- Generate visual content with AI assistance
- Price services professionally based on results, not hours

### Target Audience
- Existing community managers seeking to upskill
- Entrepreneurs managing their own social media
- Job seekers entering digital marketing
- Business owners training their marketing teams

### Brand Identity
- Company: SEO Contenidos (Venezuelan marketing agency)
- Tone: Professional yet approachable, results-focused
- Market positioning: Premium education with practical, agency-tested methodologies
